Lasagna Recipe

Momma Mia's homemade lasagna

Traditional Italian lasagna recipe inspired by Italy's finest chefs.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodiles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Layer ingredients noodle, cheese sauce, meat in baking dish
  2. Bake at 350F for 45 minutes
