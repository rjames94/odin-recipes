Pizza Recipe

Momma Mia's homemade Pizza

Traditional Italian Pizza recipe inspired by Italy's finest chefs.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Dough
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Roll dough into 1/4" thick circle
  2. Spread tomato sauce and add mozzarella, pepporini, salt, pepper
  3. Bake at 450F for 15 minutes
