Spaghetti Recipe

Momma Mia's homemade Spaghetti

Traditional Italian Spaghetti recipe inspired by Italy's finest chefs.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodiles
  • Maranara sauce
  • Parmesan
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Boil Water. Cook pasta for 11 minutes.
  2. Cook ground beef in pan at medium heat, about 10 min.
  3. Combine noodles and beef with sauce and mix, top with parmesan and basil.
